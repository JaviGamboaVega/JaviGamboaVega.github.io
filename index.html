RHOMBOID

Innovation

Our studio was built on a foundation of innovation, artistry, and hand-crafted design. We’re unlocking the true potential of online roleplaying with game-changing titles. The status quo isn’t good enough for the dedicated worldwide community of our games, which is why we question industry conventions and demand more from ourselves as developers.
